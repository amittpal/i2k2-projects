Registration Guidelines
Enter Title of your name. Title is mandatory.
Enter your First name in the space provided. The name should be the same as mentioned in Class X/XII/School leaving certificate. First name is mandatory.
Enter your Middle Name (if Applicable) in the space provided. The name should be the same as mentioned in Class X/XII/School leaving certificate.
Enter your Last Name (if Applicable) in the space provided. The name should be the same as mentioned in Class X/XII/School leaving certificate.
Enter your full address.
Select state from dropdown list.
Select city from dropdown list.
Enter your 6 digit pincode number.
Enter your Unique User Name. This User Name will be used during the entire registration process and for Login into the Candidate Portal.
Create a new password for the "Registration Portal".
Enter the same password as you entered in the "Password".
Enter your 10 digit mobile number (Do not add 0 or +91 before your number).
Enter your phone number
Enter your personal Email ID. This Email ID will be used during the entire registration process.

Note: Please check the information for any spelling mistakes before clicking 'SAVE AND VERIFY' Button.

After successful submission of the Identity Registration form, a OTP will be sent to your email address.

Please check your inbox including SPAM/BULK and submit the OTP to complete your Email Varification.
Enter your Father Name.
Enter your Date of Birth in the space provided. The Date of Birth should be the same as mentioned in Class X/XII/School leaving certificate.
Select your Gender from dropdown.
Select Physical Disability from dropdown.
Select Category from dropdown.
Select Registrations from dropdown.
Select City Priority 1 from dropdown list.
Select City Priority 2 from dropdown list.
Select City Priority 3 from dropdown list.
Select your Nationality from dropdown list.
Upload your profile photo. This Profile photo will be used in entire candidate protal.
Upload photo of your signature.

Note: Please check the information for any spelling mistakes before clicking 'SUBMIT' Button.

  • Select Payment Gateway.

  • Candidates need to have Debit Card, Credit Card or Net Banking.
